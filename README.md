# 📊 Excel - E-Commerce Annual Sales Report 

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an interactive **Microsoft Excel Dashboard** developed for **XYZ Limited** to analyze the company's annual e-commerce sales performance for **2024**.

The dashboard provides business insights into sales performance, customer demographics, delivery status, sales channels, and regional performance through dynamic visualizations and slicers.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The main objectives of this project are to:

- Analyze annual sales performance
- Monitor key business KPIs
- Understand customer purchasing behavior
- Compare sales across divisions and product categories
- Identify top-performing cities and sales channels
- Support business decision-making with interactive reports

---

# 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

| KPI | Description |
|------|-------------|
| 💰 Total Sales | Total sales generated in 2024 |
| 📦 Total Orders | Total number of customer orders in 2024 |
| 📊 Average Sales per Order | Average sales generated per order in 2024 |

---

# 📊 Dashboard Features

The dashboard includes the following business insights:

### ✅ Sales vs Orders (Month Wise)
- Monthly comparison of Total Sales and Total Orders.
- Helps identify seasonal sales trends.

### ✅ Men vs Women Sales Ratio
- Shows the percentage of sales contributed by male and female customers.

### ✅ Order Delivery Status
- Displays order distribution by:
  - Delivered
  - Returned
  - Cancelled
  - Refunded

### ✅ Top 5 Cities by Sales
- Identifies the highest revenue-generating cities.

### ✅ Orders by Age Group & Gender
- Compares customer orders across:
  - Adult
  - Teenager
  - Senior
- Split by Male and Female customers.

### ✅ Top 5 Sales Channels
- Shows which online sales channels generated the highest revenue.

### ✅ Interactive Filters (Slicers)
Users can filter the dashboard by:

- Division
- Category
